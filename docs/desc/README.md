# Store Descriptions

## Short Description (≤80 chars)

Stay in flow with a customizable Pomodoro timer, history heatmap, and themes.

## Long Description

Start focus runs in seconds, keep the screen awake, and never lose your place when you relaunch.
Customize your cadence with adjustable focus and break lengths, repeats, and long breaks, and see
changes reflected instantly in the timeline preview. Stay motivated with rotating focus quotes and
polished session controls with sound cues. Review your year at a glance via the focus history
heatmap, switch years with haptics, and tap cells to reveal daily focus and break minutes.
Preferences and history live on-device; notifications are used only for timer alerts.
